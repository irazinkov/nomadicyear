(<a href="https://www.facebook.com/groups/panamtravelers/">https://www.facebook.com/groups/panamtravelers/</a>)

Real people, real stories, happening NOW.

Join the group if you are tired of looking out of your office window.

<strong>IOverlander website and app.</strong>

(<a href="http://www.ioverlander.com">http://www.ioverlander.com</a>)

Amazing resource for finding a place to spend the night: official campgrounds, random places to sleep in the car, hotels, etc.

<h2><strong>Books</strong></h2>
<strong>"Long Way Round"</strong> by Ewan McGregor and Mr. Charley BOOOOORMAN

Actually BOUGHT and READ this book, i know hard to believe that anyone still does this.

But it was 2007, things were different back in the day.

&nbsp;

<strong>"Two Wheels Through Terror: Diary of a South American Motorcycle Odyssey"</strong> by Glen Heggstad

DO NOT read this if you are having second thoughts about this trip.

DO read this if you want to survive your trip.

&nbsp;

<strong>"Zen and the Art of Motorcycle Maintenance: An Inquiry Into Values"</strong> by Robert M. Pirsig

This shit is a classic. Spend the $7 on Amazon, treat yo self.

&nbsp;

<strong>"Lonely Planet Trans-Siberian Railway"</strong> by Simon Richmond

......"Oh, it's just 2 weeks of drinking with Russkies on the Putin train"......

Get the book, educate yourself on the beauty and expanse of Mother Russia.

&nbsp;

<strong>"Adventure Motorcycling Handbook"</strong> by Chris Scott

This is <strong>definitely</strong> going to help me fix my Land Cruiser.

&nbsp;

<strong>"One Man Caravan"</strong> by Robert Edison Fulton Jr.

God....another book about a bike? Yeah, wanted to do this journey on a bike.

Wife said "NO".
